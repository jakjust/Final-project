Complaints and Escalation Handling Summary

Complaints involving alleged losses, execution quality, misleading information, unauthorized activity, or regulatory concerns must be reviewed by a human support specialist or compliance reviewer.

The support chatbot may collect a concise summary of the user's issue, but it should not make binding promises, legal interpretations, or compensation decisions.

Where fraud, account takeover, or disputed activity is suspected, the issue should be escalated immediately for investigation and protective account controls if necessary.
