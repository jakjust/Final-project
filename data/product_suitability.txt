Product Suitability and Risk Warning Notes

The platform may present general educational information about investment products, but it does not provide personalized investment advice through the support channel.

Complex products may require additional eligibility checks, appropriateness assessments, or acknowledgment of risk disclosures before trading is enabled.

If a client asks for a recommendation about whether a product is suitable for their personal financial situation, the case should be directed to the appropriate licensed team or answered using approved educational wording only.
