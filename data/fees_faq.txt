Digital Investment Platform Fees FAQ

US stock trades are charged at USD 2.50 per executed order for standard retail accounts. Fractional share orders are charged at the same rate as whole-share orders when executed. The fee is displayed on the order preview screen before the client confirms the trade.

Currency conversion charges apply when clients trade products settled in a currency different from the base account currency. The current FX conversion spread is 0.35 percent above the reference rate.

There is no custody fee for standard accounts holding listed equities. However, corporate action handling for voluntary events may include a service fee depending on the event type and external custodian charges.

Clients should always review the latest fee schedule before submitting an order because external market charges, taxes, or regulatory levies may apply in some markets.
